About

ggpubfigs was developed by Jacob L. Steenwyk to provide publication-ready ggplot2 themes and colorblind-friendly color palettes for the life sciences.

Making scientific figures accessible to all readers, including those with color vision deficiencies, is critical for effective science communication. ggpubfigs addresses this by providing a curated set of colorblind-friendly palettes alongside clean, minimalist themes designed for publication and presentation.


The Developers

ggpubfigs is developed and maintained by Jacob L. Steenwyk.


Jacob L. Steenwyk is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Ecology, Evolution, and Organismal Biology at The Ohio State University. His research foci include understanding the parameters that influence genome stability, the genomics of microbial domestication, and the evolution of pathogenicity. Beyond research, Steenwyk aims to make education more accessible through diverse avenues of community engagement. Find out more information at his personal website.


More Team Members

ggpubfigs has been made possible by other key personnel including Antonis Rokas.


Antonis Rokas is the Cornelius Vanderbilt Chair in Biological Sciences and Director of the Evolutionary Studies Initiative at Vanderbilt University. Research in his laboratory focuses on the study of the DNA record to gain insight into the patterns and processes of evolution. Using a combination of computational and experimental approaches, his lab’s current research aims to understand the molecular foundations of the fungal lifestyle, the reconstruction of the tree of life, and the evolution of human pregnancy. Find out more information at his laboratory’s website.
